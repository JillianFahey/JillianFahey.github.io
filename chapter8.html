Chapter 8
The Gift

Please, reach out to anyone feeling alone, depressed, discriminated against, disregarded, or just sad. My guess is that they will appreciate it and you never know who will be a fit for a new friend. Think before you quickly judge, hate and/or bully someone. Life's too short to spend much time unhappy and unhappy feelings are brought upon by negative actions. Everyone can use a friend, a friend who will hold their hand, a friend who will be there for them, show compassion and kindness.

You only get one shot at a life, so make yours the best it can be. Life is a gift, cherish it with all you have and fight for yourself, your rights, your feelings, your inclusion and your love. Everything you need for a happy, successful and loving life is in your hands and is a gift within you.
